# Product Overview

**Bomba** is a comprehensive management system for cafes, restaurants, and gaming lounges with full Arabic language support.

## Core Features

### Gaming Management
- **PlayStation Sessions**: Multi-controller support with dynamic pricing (1-2 controllers: 20 EGP/hr, 3 controllers: 25 EGP/hr, 4 controllers: 30 EGP/hr)
- **Computer Sessions**: Fixed hourly rate (15 EGP/hr)
- **Real-time Tracking**: Active and completed session monitoring with controller history tracking

### Cafe Management
- **Dynamic Menu**: Product and pricing management with sections and categories
- **Order Management**: Full order lifecycle from creation to delivery
- **Custom Notes**: Per-order customization
- **Inventory Tracking**: Automatic deduction of raw materials based on menu item ingredients

### Billing System
- **Unified Bills**: Combines cafe orders and gaming sessions
- **Partial Payments**: Support for installment payments and item-specific payments
- **QR Codes**: Customer bill viewing via QR codes
- **Multiple Payment Methods**: Cash, card, and transfer support

### Reports & Analytics
- **Financial Reports**: Revenue, costs, and profit tracking
- **Sales Reports**: Best-selling items and peak hours analysis
- **Inventory Reports**: Stock status and movement tracking
- **Session Reports**: Device usage and revenue analysis

## User Roles & Permissions
- **Admin**: Full system access
- **Staff**: Limited operational access
- **Cashier**: Billing and payment management
- **Kitchen**: Order preparation and status updates

## Key Business Rules
- Sessions automatically create bills when started
- Inventory is deducted when orders are marked as "preparing"
- PlayStation pricing varies by controller count and can change mid-session
- Bills can be paid in full, partially, or by specific items
- System supports Arabic RTL interface throughout
